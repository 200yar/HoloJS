VR and AR experiences with Spin Sample script apps

Sample HoloJs experiences for VR and AR

To run the following samples on your desktop, Mixed Reality headset or HoloLens, you must side-load the Spin app from http://holojs.azurewebsites.net/spin-view. Follow the same steps for installing the certificate as outlined for installing Spin Make. If you already installed Spin Make, you do not need to install the certificate again.
Note: On the HoloLens, use the Device Portal to install the certificate and side-load the Spin package, which can be downloaded from the "Additional Links" section of the app's webpage.
Most of the samples in this section are based on ThreeJs examples, which you can find at https://threejs.org/examples/
